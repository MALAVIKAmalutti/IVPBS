Help Center - Industrial Visit Planning and Booking System

Welcome to the Industrial Visit Planning and Booking System (IVPBS)! Accessing our platform is as simple as opening your preferred web browser and navigating to our webpage. Once logged in, users can explore a range of features designed to streamline the industrial visit planning process.

IVPBS offers a convenient chat feature, allowing users to communicate directly with the company or agency handling the industrial visit arrangements. Student coordinators can easily mark attendance, ensuring accurate tracking of participants throughout the visit.

Our software includes an interactive map feature, providing users with detailed guidance on site locations and layouts. Whether planning a visit or coordinating logistics, the map feature offers invaluable assistance.

Additionally, IVPBS offers comprehensive trip guidelines, providing users with essential information and resources to facilitate smooth and successful industrial visits. From booking management to real-time communication, our platform is your all-in-one solution for efficient and effective industrial visit planning.
